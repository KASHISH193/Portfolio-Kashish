About Me

My Journey

I am a third-year Computer Science student at SZABIST Karachi, driven by a passion for building innovative, user-focused digital experiences. My journey began with a fascination for how software operates and has evolved into a commitment to crafting purposeful applications that solve real-world problems. I believe in the power of clean, functional design paired with robust engineering to make technology more accessible and meaningful.

I have hands-on experience with both frontend and backend technologies, including JavaScript, React JS, React Native, Node.js, and Express. My backend knowledge is complemented by database expertise in SQL and MongoDB. I enjoy working on full-stack projects that involve authentication, API integration, and data persistence. My technical toolkit also includes Java, Python, HTML, CSS, and version control with Git and GitHub.

In addition to development, I’m skilled in using design tools such as Figma, Canva, and Adobe Illustrator to create engaging, intuitive interfaces. I’ve built responsive websites using WordPress and have worked on projects involving UI/UX design, ensuring that my applications are not only functional but also visually compelling and user-friendly.

My portfolio includes a variety of projects: a Netflix-style landing page, a Java-based Bank Management System, a JavaScript social book app, and UI/UX designs for a self-defense mobile application.

I’m actively seeking opportunities to grow as a full-stack developer in a collaborative environment where I can apply my skills, learn from industry experts, and contribute to impactful software solutions. I value continuous learning, problem-solving, and teamwork, and aspire to be part of projects that drive innovation and improve everyday experiences through technology.
